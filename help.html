HOW TO

PLAY THIS GAME ?

  • Step 1 Press any keyboard key to start the game.
  • Step 2 Once the game has started you will be on level 1 and levels will increase as per your performance.
  • Step 3 In every level simon will glow a color and you have to correctly match the previous order as well as the color glowed in current level.
  • Step 4 If you mismatched the order of colors then ared signal will glow and the game will end. You can again restart he game by pressing any key.
